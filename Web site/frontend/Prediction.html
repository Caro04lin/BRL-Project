Prerequisites

  • Ensure you have a Wi-Fi adapter such as TP-Link. It allows you to connect two Wi-Fi networks to your laptop.
  • Connect your laptop to the Wi-Fi of the 5 x-IMUs and the Wi-Fi of the Ordro EP6 PLUS camera (5 metres maximum range):
    • IMUs wifi: define it in the x-IMU3 GUI application
      • ×
    • Camera wifi: EP6_PLUS_401229
  • Wear the 5 x-IMUs by following the position instructions provided on them and the camera around your head.
